Scan progress is how far telegram-listener has gotten through the channel's message history (its own message-ID counter, not a time estimate). Pending face-match is the separate face-worker queue — photos/video frames already downloaded but not yet matched against workers. A stuck bar or growing pending count usually means the corresponding service has hit a snag — check Admin → Logs. Reset scan restarts a channel's scan from the History Pull Limit window again (Admin → Settings) — different from skip to latest, which jumps forward past remaining backlog instead.
